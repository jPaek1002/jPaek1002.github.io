Date posted: March 9th, 2025

Week 10 of Winter 2025, Sunday.

The following excerpt is from January:

Happy new year! I was a little busy with some fun stuff such as:

  • Finding someone to replace my at my old apartment so I can move into grad housing.

  • Travelling in Japan.

  • Selling out.

I'll start with my Japan trip. I left on December 16th and came back on December 31st. My 4-years of Japanese in highschool really allowed me to free roam the place. Not too shabby. It was cool because everything was so cheap, and I didn't have to tip when I ate out.

I have recently realized that there is a little more to life than studying. Yeah, learning is cool is all, but playing PIU is really fun. To be honest, I didn't do much in fall quarter, and I think that's probably the burn out.

And that ends the short entry. I have fallen behind on a lot of work, but I have realized that I need to strongly reconsider the people I choose to be around. I have grow defensive and succumbed to the sunk cost fallacy of a relationship, when truly I am the one being drained. Not just of energy, but my precious time. In such scenarios, it is important to take action. The severity of the action is my choice, and I have chosen to make a drastic one. I look forward to pursue further endeavors as I rid my path of obstacles.

“Pain happens when you care”
